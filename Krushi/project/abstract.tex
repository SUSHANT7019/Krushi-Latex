{\paragraph{}Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian economy, but farmers often face challenges such as crop
diseases, improper soil management, and limited access to agricultural resources. The Krushi
website aims to provide a one-stop digital solution for farmers by integrating plant disease
detection, soil analysis, an online shop, and an AI-powered chatbot. Using machine learning
and web technologies, Krushi helps farmers identify plant diseases through image processing, analyze soil health, purchase farming products, and receive instant agricultural guidance. This
system enhances productivity, reduces crop loss, and empowers farmers with smart digital tools
for sustainable agriculture.
