\chapter{Conclusion}

\paragraph{} The Krushi system was developed as an integrated smart agriculture assistance platform that provided farmers with plant disease detection, soil analysis, chatbot-based advisory support, and an online agricultural marketplace through a single digital interface. The system helped farmers identify crop diseases at an early stage, receive soil-based crop recommendations, and access agricultural guidance conveniently. By promoting technology-based decision-making and improving accessibility to farming resources, the Krushi platform contributed toward enhanced productivity, reduced crop losses, and sustainable agricultural development.
